Common Questions

FAQ

Does Alfredo's Tire Shop offer used tires?

Yes! We sell both new and quality used tires for cars, trucks, and SUVs at affordable prices. Mounting and balancing are included with every tire purchase.

Is Alfredo's Tire Shop open on Sunday?

Yes, we're open on Sundays from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM. We are closed on Saturdays. Monday through Friday hours are 8:00 AM to 5:30 PM.

Do you do oil changes?

Yes, we offer quick and affordable oil changes. No appointment needed — just drive in during business hours.

Se habla español?

¡Sí! We proudly serve both English and Spanish speaking customers in the Cleveland, TN area.

Do I need an appointment?

No appointment necessary. Walk-ins are welcome for all services including tire installation, oil changes, and general maintenance.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Cleveland, TN and the surrounding areas including Ooltewah, Collegedale, Athens, Benton, and Chattanooga.
